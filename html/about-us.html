Our Mission

At BIT Mesra, we understand the importance of efficient transportation in ensuring smooth operations and facilitating the mobility of our university community. Our mission with the Transport Management System is to provide a reliable, safe, and convenient transportation experience for all users.

Features

  • Real-time Tracking: Our TMS offers real-time tracking of university buses, allowing users to know the exact location and estimated arrival time of the buses.
  • Route Information: Detailed information about bus routes, stops, and schedules is provided to assist users in planning their journeys effectively.
  • User-Friendly Interface: The system is designed with a user-friendly interface, making it easy for users to navigate and access the information they need.
  • Feedback Mechanism: We value feedback from our users to continually improve our transportation services. The TMS includes a feedback mechanism for users to provide suggestions and report any issues they encounter.

How to Use

  1. Log in using your BIT Mesra credentials.
  2. Choose your desired route and bus stop.
  3. Track the real-time location of the buses.
  4. Provide feedback to help us improve our services.
